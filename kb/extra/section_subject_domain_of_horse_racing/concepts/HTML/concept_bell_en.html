Bell - The bell is designed for signals to participants, for example: the next participant prepare for access to the site:
* Give a signal for the beginning of the performance
* Stop the speaker
* Give the participant permission to continue the performance after his stop
* Disqualification of the participant (long hit or 3 strokes)
* If the participant did not stop after the signal of the stop, it can be disqualified. The resumption of the performance after the stop, without waiting for the corresponding signal, also leads to disqualification.
